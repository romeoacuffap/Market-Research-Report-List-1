<p><p>The Veterinary Trocars market is categorized into three main types: orthopedic, thoracic, and laparoscopic. Orthopedic trocars are used in procedures related to bones and joints, facilitating drainage and access during surgeries. Thoracic trocars are designed for chest procedures, allowing for fluid drainage and diagnostic sampling. Laparoscopic trocars enable minimally invasive surgeries in various veterinary fields, providing access to the abdominal cavity while minimizing incisions and recovery time, thereby enhancing animal welfare and surgical outcomes.</p></p>
