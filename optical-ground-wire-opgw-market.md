<p><strong>The Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) serves a dual purpose in telecommunications and power transmission, integrating optical fibers with electrical grounding functions. The Central Tube Structure OPGW features a central tube housing the optical fibers, surrounded by aluminum-alloy wires, providing enhanced strength and protection. In contrast, the Layer Stranding Structure OPGW consists of multiple layers of optical fibers stranded between aluminum conductors, allowing for increased flexibility and improved thermal performance. Both types cater to varying installation environments and engineering requirements in the market.</p></p>
