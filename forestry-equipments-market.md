<p><ul><li>Felling Equipment</li><li>Extracting Equipment</li><li>On-Site Processing Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forestry equipment market comprises three key types: felling equipment, extracting equipment, and on-site processing equipment. Felling equipment includes tools and machinery used to cut down trees, such as chainsaws and feller bunchers. Extracting equipment focuses on the removal of harvested timber from the forest, including skidders and forwarders. On-site processing equipment involves machinery that converts felled trees into usable products, such as chippers and sawmills, enhancing efficiency and productivity in the forestry industry.</p></p>
