<p><p>A Positive Displacement Motor (PDM) is a type of hydraulic motor that converts hydraulic pressure into mechanical energy through the direct displacement of fluid within its chambers. It operates by trapping a fixed volume of fluid and forcing it out, generating torque and enabling precise control over speed and output. These motors are widely used in various industries including construction, agriculture, and manufacturing due to their efficiency and high torque at low speeds.</p><p>The Positive Displacement Motor Market is expected to experience significant growth, driven by increasing demand for efficient machinery across sectors.
