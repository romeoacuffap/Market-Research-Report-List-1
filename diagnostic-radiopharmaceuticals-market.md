<p><p>The diagnostic radiopharmaceuticals market is primarily categorized into three types: Technetium-99, Fluorine-18, and others. Technetium-99 is widely used for various imaging procedures due to its favorable properties, including optimal half-life and minimal radiation exposure. Fluorine-18 is essential for positron emission tomography (PET) scans, particularly in oncology and neurology, owing to its effectiveness in highlighting metabolic processes. Other radiopharmaceuticals include iodine-123 and gallium-68, which serve specialized applications in diagnostics, enhancing the overall imaging capabilities in medical practice.</p></p>
