<p><p>The nanometer zirconium dioxide market is categorized into three primary phases: monoclinic, tetragonal, and cubic. The monoclinic phase, stable at room temperature, is known for its high strength and wear resistance, making it suitable for ceramics. The tetragonal phase offers enhanced toughness and is commonly used in dental applications and advanced ceramics. The cubic phase, while less stable, provides superior transparency and electrical properties, making it ideal for optoelectronic devices and bioapplications. Each phase targets specific industries based on its unique properties.</p></p>
