<p><p>The Polyvinyl Acetate (PVA) emulsion market is categorized into two main types: General Grade and Modified Grade. General Grade PVA emulsion is commonly used in adhesives, paints, and coatings due to its excellent bonding properties and versatility. In contrast, Modified Grade PVA emulsion is enhanced with additives to improve specific characteristics, such as water resistance, flexibility, or UV stability. This makes it suitable for specialized applications, including construction materials, woodworking, and textile industries, where enhanced performance is required.</p></p>
