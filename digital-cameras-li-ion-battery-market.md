<p><ul><li>Liquified Lithium-Ion Battery</li><li>Polymer Lithium-Ion Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital camera Li-ion battery market primarily features two types: Liquified Lithium-Ion Batteries and Polymer Lithium-Ion Batteries. Liquified Lithium-Ion Batteries use liquid electrolyte, offering high energy density and efficiency, making them suitable for compact cameras. Polymer Lithium-Ion Batteries, on the other hand, utilize solid polymer electrolytes, providing a lightweight and flexible design with enhanced safety. These variants cater to different consumer needs, with liquified types favored for performance and polymer types preferred for portability and safety in modern digital photography.</p></p>
