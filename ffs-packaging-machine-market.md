<p><p>The FFS (Form-Fill-Seal) packaging machine market is segmented into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical FFS machines operate by forming bags from a roll of film, filling them with product, and sealing them in a vertical orientation, making them suitable for liquids or powders. In contrast, horizontal FFS machines create packages in a horizontal format, ideal for bulk or solid items, offering versatility in packaging styles. Both types optimize efficiency and automation in packing processes across various industries.</p></p>
