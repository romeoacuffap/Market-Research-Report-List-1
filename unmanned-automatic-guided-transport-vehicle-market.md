<p><p>The Unmanned Automatic Guided Transport Vehicle market consists of various guidance types: Electromagnetic Induction Guidance uses electromagnetic fields to direct vehicles along predefined paths, ensuring precision in navigation. Laser Guidance employs laser beams for position tracking, allowing for flexible route adjustments and high accuracy. Magnetic-Gyro Guidance combines magnetic fields with gyroscopic sensors to maintain stability and direction, providing robust navigation in complex environments. Each guidance method enhances operational efficiency and safety in automated transport systems.</p></p>
