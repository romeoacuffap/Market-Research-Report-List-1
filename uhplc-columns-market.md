<p><p>The UHPLC (Ultra-High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) columns market comprises two primary types: packed columns and capillary columns. Packed columns feature densely packed particles, enhancing separation efficiency for complex mixtures, making them suitable for various applications in pharmaceuticals and biochemistry. Capillary columns, characterized by their small inner diameters, provide high resolution and sensitivity, ideal for trace analysis and micro-sampling. Both column types play crucial roles in improving analytical performance and precision in various industries, including healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p></p>
