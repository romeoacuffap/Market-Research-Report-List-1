<p><p>The two primary types of metal detectors in the market are electromagnetic induction and x-ray/microwave systems. Electromagnetic induction detectors use varying magnetic fields to identify metal objects by inducing currents, making them effective for security screening and industrial applications. In contrast, x-ray and microwave detectors utilize beams to penetrate materials, detecting hidden metallic objects by analyzing the emitted radiation patterns. Both types cater to distinct needs, offering reliable solutions for security, contamination detection, and quality control across various industries.</p></p>
