<p><p>The infrared motion sensor market encompasses two primary types: Passive Infrared Sensors (PIR) and Imaging Infrared Sensors. PIR sensors detect motion by measuring changes in infrared radiation, typically emitted by people or animals, making them ideal for security and automation applications. In contrast, Imaging Infrared Sensors capture thermal images based on temperature variations, providing more detailed visuals and enabling advanced monitoring capabilities. Both types serve various sectors, including security, automotive, and consumer electronics, enhancing safety and energy efficiency.</p></p>
