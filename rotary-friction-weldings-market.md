<p><p>The Rotary Friction Welding market encompasses three primary types: Inertia Rotary Friction Welding, which utilizes rotational inertia to generate heat and forge materials together; Direct Drive Rotary Friction Welding, where the rotary motion is directly powered by a motor, offering precise control and quick cycles; and Hybrid Rotary Friction Welding, which combines elements of both inertia and direct drive methods for enhanced flexibility and performance. Each type serves specific applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
