<p><p>The frozen food packaging market is primarily divided into two types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging involves materials like pouches and bags that can easily adapt to the shape of the contents, providing better sealing and reduced space. It is lightweight and often more sustainable. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, includes containers like jars, tins, and boxes, offering durability and protection for fragile items. Both types play crucial roles in preserving food quality and extending shelf life.</p></p>
