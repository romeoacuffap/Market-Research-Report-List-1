<p><p>The digital microsensor market encompasses three main types: chemical microsensors, biological microsensors, and physical microsensors. Chemical microsensors detect specific chemical substances, enabling applications in environmental monitoring and industrial processes. Biological microsensors, on the other hand, identify biological components, serving vital roles in healthcare and diagnostics. Physical microsensors measure parameters like temperature, pressure, and humidity, finding use in various industries, including automotive and aerospace. Together, these microsensors enhance precision and efficiency in detecting and analyzing diverse phenomena.</p></p>
