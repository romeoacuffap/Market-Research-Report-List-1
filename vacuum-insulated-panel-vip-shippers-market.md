<p><p>Vacuum Insulated Panel (VIP) Shippers are advanced thermal insulation materials designed to minimize heat transfer and maintain temperature stability for sensitive products during transportation. VIP shippers are increasingly utilized in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food and beverage, where maintaining specific temperature ranges is crucial.</p><p>The VIP shippers market is witnessing substantial growth, driven by the rising demand for efficient and sustainable packaging solutions.
