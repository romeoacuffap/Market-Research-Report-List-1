<p><p>The High Temperature Heat Recovery Wheel market consists of three main types: Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. Condensation Rotors focus on recovering heat through phase change from vapor to liquid, optimizing energy efficiency. Hygroscopic Rotors utilize materials that absorb moisture to improve humidity control and energy performance. Sorption Rotors combine heat and mass transfer technologies, enabling effective energy recovery in varying temperatures and conditions. Each type caters to specific applications in industrial processes for enhanced energy management.</p></p>
