<p><ul><li> Roof Prism Binoculars</li><li>Porro Prism Binoculars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lightweight binoculars market primarily features two types: roof prism binoculars and Porro prism binoculars. Roof prism binoculars are characterized by their compact, straight-line design, making them portable and ideal for outdoor activities. They typically offer a more modern aesthetic and are easier to handle. In contrast, Porro prism binoculars have a wider shape with an offset eyepiece and objective lenses, providing a greater depth of field and superior light transmission, enhancing overall image quality. Each type caters to different user preferences and requirements.</p></p>
