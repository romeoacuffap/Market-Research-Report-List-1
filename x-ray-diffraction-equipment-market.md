<p><p>The X-ray Diffraction (XRD) equipment market comprises two primary types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD analyzes crystalline materials in powdered form, providing information on crystal structures and phases, making it ideal for materials like ceramics and minerals. In contrast, Single-crystal XRD examines individual crystalline samples, yielding detailed insights into molecular arrangements and symmetries, crucial for characterizing complex compounds. Together, these techniques serve diverse applications in material science, chemistry, and biology, enhancing our understanding of material properties.</p></p>
