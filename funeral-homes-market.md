<p><p>Funeral homes operate primarily in two market types: at-need and pre-need. The at-need market involves services provided immediately following a death, where families make urgent decisions regarding arrangements. Conversely, the pre-need market allows individuals to plan and pay for their funeral services in advance, easing the burden on loved ones during a difficult time. Both markets play crucial roles in the funeral industry, addressing different consumer needs and preferences related to end-of-life planning.</p></p>
