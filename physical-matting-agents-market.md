<p><p>Physical matting agents are additives used to reduce gloss and improve the surface texture of coatings and inks. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Silica Matting Agents**: These are fine, amorphous silica particles that provide excellent transparency while effectively matting surfaces. </p><p>2. **Wax Matting Agents**: These are typically made from natural or synthetic waxes that not only reduce gloss but also offer improved scratch resistance and slip properties.</p><p>3. **Other Matting Agents**: This category encompasses various materials like talc and starch, which provide unique textures and functionality based on application needs.</p></p>
