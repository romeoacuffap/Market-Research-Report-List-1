<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Ternary Battery</li><li>Cylindrical Lithium Iron Phosphate Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cylindrical power battery market includes two main types: cylindrical ternary batteries and cylindrical lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries. Cylindrical ternary batteries utilize a combination of nickel, cobalt, and manganese in their cathodes, offering high energy density and efficiency, making them ideal for electric vehicles and portable electronics. In contrast, cylindrical lithium iron phosphate batteries provide enhanced thermal stability and safety, with longer life cycles, mainly used in applications requiring robust performance, such as renewable energy storage and power tools.</p></p>
