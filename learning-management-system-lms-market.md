<p><p>A Learning Management System (LMS) is a software platform designed to deliver, track, and manage educational courses and training programs. The market for LMS solutions can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based LMS solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easier accessibility for users. In contrast, on-premise LMS solutions are installed locally on an organization’s servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring more maintenance and IT resources.</p></p>
