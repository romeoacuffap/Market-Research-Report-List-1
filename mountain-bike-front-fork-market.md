<p><strong>The Mountain Bike Front Fork Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Spring Suspension Fork</li><li>Coil Spring Suspension Fork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mountain bike front fork market is primarily divided into two types: air spring suspension forks and coil spring suspension forks. Air spring forks utilize air pressure to provide a lightweight and adjustable ride, allowing for fine-tuning based on rider preferences and terrain. In contrast, coil spring forks rely on a metal spring for cushioning, offering consistent performance and simplicity, but are generally heavier. Each type caters to different riding styles, preferences, and budget considerations, influencing bike performance and comfort.</p></p>
