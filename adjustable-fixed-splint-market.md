<p><p>The adjustable fixed splint market encompasses three primary types: head splints, limb splints, and torso splints. Head splints are designed to immobilize and stabilize cranial injuries, ensuring protection and support during recovery. Limb splints cater to fractures and strains in arms and legs, facilitating proper alignment and healing. Torso splints are used for spinal and rib injuries, providing essential support to the upper body. Each type is tailored to enhance patient recovery and comfort while maintaining structural integrity.</p></p>
