<p><strong>The Blood Irradiation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiation equipment market is categorized into two primary types: X-ray and gamma-ray blood irradiation equipment. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to reduce the risk of transfusion-related complications by inactivating pathogens in blood products. Gamma-ray blood irradiation, on the other hand, employs gamma radiation from sources like cobalt-60 to achieve similar results, effectively sterilizing blood components. Both technologies are essential for ensuring the safety and quality of blood transfusions, contributing to improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
