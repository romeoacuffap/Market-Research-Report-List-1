<p><p>The silicon wafer cutting equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: diamond-coated wire and steel wire. Diamond-coated wire utilizes abrasive diamond particles for precise cutting, offering superior efficiency and longevity, making it suitable for high-quality wafer production. In contrast, steel wire is a more cost-effective option, commonly employed for standard applications where high precision is less critical. Both types serve essential roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process, balancing performance with cost-effectiveness based on production requirements.</p></p>
