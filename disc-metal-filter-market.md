<p><p>The Disc Metal Filter market is categorized into two main types: Wire Mesh and Laser Drilled Holes. Wire Mesh filters consist of woven metal strands that offer excellent durability and a range of filtration levels, catering to various applications. In contrast, Laser Drilled Holes filters utilize precision laser technology to create uniform, fine holes, allowing for precise filtration and reduced clogging. Both types serve industries such as oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment, ensuring efficient separation and purification processes.</p></p>
