<p><p>Digital door lock systems encompass various types, including magnetic stripe locks, electromechanical door locks, electric strike locks, and biometric locks. Magnetic stripe locks use encoded cards for access, while electromechanical door locks combine mechanical locking mechanisms with electronic controls for enhanced security. Electric strike locks replace traditional strikes to enable remote unlocking. Biometric locks utilize unique identifiers like fingerprints or facial recognition for access, providing advanced security and convenience. Together, these systems cater to diverse security needs across residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
