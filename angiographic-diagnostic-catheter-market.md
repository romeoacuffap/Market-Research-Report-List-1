<p><ul><li>Cutting Balloon Catheters</li><li>Drug-eluting Balloon Catheters</li><li>Scoring Balloon Catheters</li><li>Conventional Balloon Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiographic diagnostic catheter market includes various specialized balloon catheters tailored for vascular interventions. Cutting balloon catheters feature blades that make incisions in the plaque, facilitating easier vessel dilation. Drug-eluting balloon catheters deliver medication to prevent restenosis while expanding the artery. Scoring balloon catheters use micro-knife technologies to score plaque, enhancing dilation efficiency. Conventional balloon catheters are basic inflatable devices used to open narrowed arteries. Each type addresses specific clinical needs in treating cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
