<p><p>The ultrasonic transmitter market comprises various types, including Doppler type, sound field type, and others. Doppler type transmitters utilize the Doppler effect to measure speed and distance by detecting frequency shifts in reflected waves, making them ideal for motion detection. Sound field type transmitters focus on generating specific sound patterns for applications like location tracking and obstacle detection. Other types may include specialized devices designed for niche applications, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, industrial, and healthcare sectors.</p></p>
