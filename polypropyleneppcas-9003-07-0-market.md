<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) is a versatile thermoplastic polymer widely used in various applications. It is categorized into three main types: isotactic polypropylene (iPP), known for its high strength and transparency, is commonly used in packaging and automotive parts; syndiotactic polypropylene (sPP) offers excellent thermal resistance and is used in fibers and non-woven fabrics; atactic polypropylene (aPP), characterized by its amorphous structure, is mainly utilized as a rubber-like material in adhesives and sealants. Each type has distinct properties, facilitating diverse market applications.</p></p>
