<p><p>The potentiometer market is primarily categorized into two types: rotary and linear potentiometers. Rotary potentiometers feature a circular shaft that allows for variable resistance control as it rotates, commonly used in audio equipment and various electronic devices. Conversely, linear potentiometers utilize a sliding mechanism to vary resistance linearly along a straight path, often employed in applications such as volume controls or position sensing. Both types are essential for adjusting electrical signals in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
