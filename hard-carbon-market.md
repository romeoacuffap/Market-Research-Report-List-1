<p><p>Hard carbon is typically derived from various sources, each catering to specific industrial applications. Natural plant-based hard carbon, sourced from biomass like coconut shells, is valued for its renewable nature and eco-friendliness. Resin-based hard carbon is created from synthetic resins, providing tailored properties for niche applications. Other sources may include pyrolyzed organic materials, contributing to the diversity of hard carbon products in the market.</p></p>
