<p><p>Accounts Payable (AP) automation encompasses two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises AP automation involves software installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based AP automation operates through the internet, providing scalable solutions with lower upfront costs and easier updates. Cloud offerings facilitate remote access, collaboration, and integration with other tools, making them increasingly popular among businesses seeking flexibility and efficiency in managing accounts payable processes.</p></p>
